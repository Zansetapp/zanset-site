Privacy Policy

Sa nou ranmase

What we collect

Nou ka ranmase done debaz (non, imèl), done itilizasyon, ak kontni ou pataje. Nou pa vann done pèsonèl.

We may collect basic info (name, email), usage data, and content you share. We do not sell personal data.

Kijan nou itilize done yo

How we use data

  • Pou bay sèvis la, amelyore pèfòmans, epi sipòte kont ou.
  • Pou voye mizajou, sekirite, ak nouvèl enpòtan.
  • Pou analiz estatistik san idantifyan pèsonèl.
  • To deliver the service, improve performance, and support your account.
  • To send updates, security notices, and important news.
  • For aggregate analytics without personal identifiers.

Pataje ak twazyèm pati

Sharing with third parties

Nou pataje done sèlman ak founisè konfyans (hosting, analytics, peman) anba akò pwoteksyon done.

We share data only with trusted vendors (hosting, analytics, payments) under data-protection agreements.

Sekirite

Security

Nou itilize mezi endistri a (HTTPS, kontwòl aksè, sovgad). Si ta gen ensidan, n ap notifye w jan lalwa mande.

We use industry measures (HTTPS, access controls, backups). If an incident occurs, we’ll notify you as required by law.
